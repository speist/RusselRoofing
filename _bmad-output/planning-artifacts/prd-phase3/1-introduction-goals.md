# 1. Introduction & Goals

**Phase 3 — Analytics & SEO Enhancements** builds on the completed Phase 1 (Epics 1–4) and Phase 2
(Epics 5–7) work. With the site live and functional, this phase captures the discoverability and
measurement opportunities identified in the analytics/SEO research
(`../technical-research-analytics-seo.md`, `../RR-analytics-seo-recommendation-CLIENT.md`).

**Goals:**
- Make Russell Roofing's existing FAQ and gallery content machine-readable so it is eligible for rich
  results and AI-answer sourcing.
- Stand up privacy-friendly, zero-recurring-cost analytics so SEO improvements are measurable.
- Keep every change set-and-forget: driven from existing data sources, with no new content maintenance
  burden for the client.
